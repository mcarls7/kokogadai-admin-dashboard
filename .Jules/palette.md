## 2026-05-30 - Contribution UX in Boilerplate Projects
**Learning:** In repositories that are essentially empty boilerplates, the documentation (README and PR templates) serves as the primary "user interface" for developers. Enhancing these with navigation anchors and clear checklists significantly improves the initial Developer Experience (DX).
**Action:** Prioritize scannability and clear contribution guardrails when a project is in its early stages.
