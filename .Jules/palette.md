## 2026-06-29 - Contribution UX for Boilerplate Repositories
**Learning:** In projects with no functional UI, the primary "interface" is the repository structure and documentation. Improving the scannability of READMEs and providing clear PR templates significantly reduces friction for first-time contributors and ensures a baseline for accessibility from day one.
**Action:** Implement structured navigation, explicit anchors, and a compact UX-focused PR template to establish a professional and accessible contribution environment.
