## 2026-05-30 - Contribution UX in Boilerplate Projects
**Learning:** In repositories lacking a functional UI, the documentation (README, PR templates) serves as the primary interface. Applying UX principles like scannability, clear navigation anchors, and concise checklists improves the developer experience (DX) and ensures accessibility standards are considered from the start.
**Action:** Use emoji-prefixed headers, explicit HTML anchors for navigation, and compact PR checklists to maintain high density and low friction.
